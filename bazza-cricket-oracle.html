About

Bazza's Cricket Oracle pulls live data from CricketData.org (formerly CricAPI). Free tier: 100 hits/day. Data is manually entered by the CricAPI team and typically lags by ~1 minute. Built for casual cricket checking — Test, ODI & T20 — when there's literally nothing else on. 🏏
